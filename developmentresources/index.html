Schematron

Schematron definitions used to validate requests to IHE/XDR HL7 CDA interface to the Danish National Patient Register are available for integrators. The entry point Schematron definition is called lpr-lpr3fr.sch.

Nightly builds

Nightly builds are available in the unstable branch of CDA resources for the Danish National Patient Register
